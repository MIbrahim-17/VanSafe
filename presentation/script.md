# VanSafe — Video Script (open & close)

_Tone: calm, confident, personal. Look at the camera for these lines._

## 🎬 Opening (~20–25s)

> "Every school morning, my parents had the same worry — the van's late, the driver isn't picking up, and there's no way to know where my sibling actually is.
>
> In a city of heavy traffic, 45-degree heat, and unverified drivers, that worry is something millions of Pakistani families live with daily.
>
> So I built **VanSafe** — a platform that turns a blind, informal school-van ride into a tracked, trusted one. Let me show you."

_→ switch to the live app._

## 🎬 Closing (~20–25s)

> "So that's VanSafe — parents find and track trusted vans, drivers build real credibility, and everyone stays informed.
>
> But the real prize is the data underneath: the busiest school-run routes, where unverified and over-capacity vans operate, fuel use across the city — insights we can hand to traffic authorities and emergency services to make the whole city safer.
>
> It starts with one family's worry. It scales into a city's safety infrastructure.
>
> Thank you."

---

**One-line hook (if you need a tighter open):**
> "This project started at my own front door — with a school van that was always late, and a driver who never picked up."

**One-line close (if you need a tighter end):**
> "Safer for one child today; smarter for every child tomorrow. That's VanSafe."
